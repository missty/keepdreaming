CELUV

Mobile Application (iOS) & Manager Website

CELUV is a mobile application that helps fans stay updated with their favorite celebrities.

As one of the founding members, I participated right from the early stage of the company. The company Celuv is a startup company founded with the vision to become the next generation entertainment agency. It was initially started with the idea to provide fans with information on upcoming concerts that they might be interested in. However, after user research and feedbacks from VCs and mentors, we pivoted our product to provide fans with updates on their favorite celebrities. Now, the company aims to help fans to stay closer to their favorite celebrities, and help agencies to manage the celebrities and their schedules more effectively. As the Chief Product Officer, I led and managed the development of the Celuv mobile application and the Manager website. I also created the UX strategies, conducted user research and IA, and produced wireframes and prototypes for both the mobile app and the manager website.

Mobile Application

For the user research, interviews and surveys were conducted to understand different needs of users according to their levels of enthusiasm toward their favorite celebrities, from casual fans to fanatics. Users tend to like multiple celebrities from singers, actors, athletes, models, youtubers, to even politicians. And due to the different types of celebrities, thei information were scattered across multiple media channels, and fans had to spend significant amount of their time in order to stay updated with the upcoming events of these celebrities.
The MVP focused on satisfying this key user need. (The app is currently under testing.)

Manager Website

During my last two months with Celuv, I focused on building the Manager Website with which agencies and celebrities can manage their schedules. Based on the personas, user stories and user flows I created, I drew out the wireframes for the Manager Website which aims to provide agencies and managers with an easy and convenient tool to manage their celebrities and their schedules.

JusTrip

Mobile Application (Android) & Admin Website

JusTrip was developed to ensure the Chinese tourists traveling in Korea with a fun yet a safe trip. It provided custom travel plans according to the length of stay, and while traveling, users could easily contact or find the location of necessary facilities in case of emergency (e.g. hospital, police station, etc.)

As the Project Manager, I oversaw the work of designers and developers in developing the JusTrip mobile app and the admin website. I created IA documents to facilitate the communication between outsourced designers and developers. I also planned and implemented UX and marketing strategies for JusTrip, and created business plan, user scenarios, flow charts, IA documents, wireframes, and interactive prototype.

Kakao Autonomous Taxi Service Design

with Kakao Mobility

Purpose of the project was to design a user-centered service for the autonomous taxi. While other projects on the market focused on the autonomous vehicle technology, the focus of our project was on the service aspect.

First, we drew the service blueprint by identifying all the touchpoints and designed the interaction for each touchpoint.

Service Blueprint

Then we rented a car and equipped it with the prototypes we designed. Different prototyping methods were used in order to provide the users a realistic experience of an autonomous vehicle. Pilot tests were conducted to improve the design.

Finally, we recruited testers and conducted the test on actual users. Users were interviewed before and after the taxi ride, and they were asked to call and get on/off the taxi as they are used to. They also answered a survey on the emotion they felt during each step of the journey.
